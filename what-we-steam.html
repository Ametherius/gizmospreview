What We Steam

If it can be cleaned, we can steam it!

At Gizmo’s Steam Services, we specialize in powerful, chemical-free steam cleaning that’s safe, effective, and eco-conscious. Our services are fully mobile — we come to you, fully equipped and ready to restore, refresh, and sanitize just about anything.

Residential Steam Cleaning

  • Kitchens: countertops, stovetops, ovens, range hoods, grout, backsplashes
  • Bathrooms: toilets, tubs, showers, tile grout, sinks, glass, fixtures.
  • Floors & Baseboards: tile, vinyl, hardwood-safe steaming, trim
  • Furniture & Upholstery: Chairs, couches, kids car seats, pet beds
  • Drapes & Blinds: deep steam that lifts dust and bacteria without harsh chemicals
  • Mattresses: deep sanitizing for dust mites, allergens, and odours

Specialty & Itemized Cleaning

  • Commercial kitchens & Hood vents
  • BBQs & Outdoor Grills: carbon removal without wire brushes or chemicals
  • Luggage & Bags: disinfected and deodorized — great after travel
  • Pet Crates & Carriers: sanitized without residue
  • Wheelchairs & Walkers: gently but thoroughly cleaned
  • Rental Move-In/Out Cleans: full-spectrum sanitization and deodorizing
